Spaghetti

Description

A simple and tasty spaghetti recipe!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meatballs
  • Spaghetti Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until they are al dente
  2. Heat up the sauce in a pan on medium
  3. Add the meatballs
  4. Strain the noodles
  5. Add noodles to plate
  6. Add sauce to noodles
  7. EAT THAT SHIT
