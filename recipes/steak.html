Steak

Description

It's just steak bruh

Ingredients

  • 48lb Tomahawk Ribeye
  • Butter
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Add salt to the steak
  2. Heat a Cast Iron pan to extreme
  3. Add butter to pan
  4. Shovel the steak into the pan
  5. Add more butter and thyme
  6. Baste the steak with the thyme butter
  7. Cook 2 minutes on each side
  8. Let rest for 4 minutes
  9. Cut, Plate, and Enjoy!
